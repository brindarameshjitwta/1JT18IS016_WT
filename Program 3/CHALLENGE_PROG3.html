Malpe Beach,Udupi



By Brinda Ramesh

     
Malpe is a natural port in the Udupi District in Karnataka,India.Located on the mouth of the Malpe River about six kilometers to the west of Udupi,it is an important port and a major fishing harbor on the Karnataka coast.The town of Malpe is associated largely with settelments of the Mogaveera fisherman community
